Oh, hi. This is Comics on a Budget.
How to use it:

  1. click on New Comic
  2. insert the comic name and price of the comic you want to buy
  3. click the "Insert!" button
  4. repeat
  5. set the price and click "What can I afford?"
The selected lines are the comics you can buy, staying in budget.
